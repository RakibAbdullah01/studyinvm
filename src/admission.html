How to apply for Scholarship

1

Choose Program & University

    You can find university and program from our website.:

  • Choose University
  • Choose Program
2

Choose Program & University

    After choosing University & Program you have to prapare all those documents:

  • Passport Information Page
  • Photo(white background)
  • Height Degree Certificate
  • Height Degree Transcripts
  • English Test Report(if have)
  • Police Clearance
  • Medical Test Report(General medical report of immigration)
  • Application Form (Download here)
3

Get Admission Notice & Visa Application

After review your application by university you will get admission notice and visa application.

4

Get your Visa & Fly for brighfuture

There is three options to get Visa:

  • Apply Visa at Vietnam Embassy: You can go to embassy for get your visa.
  • Apply Visa On Arrival(VOA): Vietnam visa-on-arrival is a process of obtaining the pre-approved visa letter (visa approval letter) through a travel agency in Vietnam to check in at the departure airport and pick up the actual visa stamp at an international airport in Vietnam upon arrival. You can follow the link: VISA ON ARRIVAL
  • E-Visa: E-visa avablable for some countries. You can follow the link: Apply for E-Visa
