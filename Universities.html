Universities

Vietnam Maritime University

  • Vietnam Maritime University (VIMARU) - public higher education institution in Vietnam. VIMARU opened its doors in 1956. Many independent academic rankings put Vietnam Maritime University in the top 30 best universities in Vietnam.
  • The composition of the university. Vietnam Maritime University is medium in scale, teaching no more than 15 thousand students at a time. 499 specialists are employed by the university. The university has international partners which allows its students to participate in exchange programs.
  • Infrastructure of VIMARU. The library is available to all of the students. There are facilities in place that allow the students to participate in sports of their liking.

Ton Duc Thang University

  • Ton Duc Thang University (TDTU) is a public and the biggest university in Vietnam. TDTU is ranked 101st – 200th in 2019 THE University Impact Rankings and be in TOP 901st – 1000th universities in the world (by ARWU, 2019.
  • Ton Duc Thang University - higher education institution in Vietnam. The main campus of the university is located in Ho Chi Minh City. Many independent academic rankings put Ton Duc Thang University in the top 20 best universities in Vietnam.

University of Economics and Finance (UEF)

  • University of Economics and Finance, Ho Chi Minh City or Truong Dai hoc Kinh te Tai chinh Thanh pho Ho Chi Minh – Ho Chi Minh City University of Economics and Finance (UEF) - private higher education institution in Vietnam. In 2007 University of Economics and Finance, Ho Chi Minh City opened its door to students. The campus is located in Ho Chi Minh City. University of Economics and Finance, Ho Chi Minh City is one of the top educational institutions in Vietnam that never leaves the country’s top 5 places to study. University of Economics and Finance, Ho Chi Minh City ranks in the national rating of Vietnam, this impressive result was achieved in less then 15 years.

Hongbang International University

  • Hongbang International University or Truong Dai học Quoc te Hong Bang (HIU) - private higher education institution in Vietnam. Hongbang International University was officially founded in 1997. In Ho Chi Minh City the main building of the university is located. Hongbang International University is one of the top educational institutions in Vietnam that never leaves the country’s top 5 places to study. Despite being one of the youngest universities of Vietnam, Hongbang International University ranks high in international ratings.
